Cian and Jack Desmond are twin filmmakers from Cork, Ireland. Together, their films have been selected for film festivals across Europe and North America. They have won awards for their work, including Best Short Form and Best Camerawork at the Royal Television Society awards (2020).

Their Cork based creative video agency offers high-end video production throughout Ireland - for narrative, music video, commercial, and corporate.
