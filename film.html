Films.

Desmond Bros Media is an award-winning film production company in Cork, Ireland. Their films and documentaries have screened at film festivals around Europe and the world.

SELECTED FILMOGRAPHY

  • Simon (2021)

    Al Jazeera 50 minute TV documentary. Ireland unit provided by Desmond Bros.

    Ireland Unit - Director/DOP: Jack Desmond. Sound: Cian Desmond.

    Watch full documentary.

  • In Need of Repair (2020)

    An isolated elderly man and a lonely repairman form an unlikely friendship.

    Written & dir. by Jack Desmond. Produced by Philip Emo. Edited by Cian Desmond.

  • Looking For (2019)

    In a world where love is revealed by rings, a lonely young woman struggles to fill what's missing in her life, before discovering things are not as simple as she had thought.

    Winner, Best Short Form - Royal Television Society awards ROI 2020. Winner, Best Cinematography, RTS awards ROI 2020.

    National TV Premiere - Virgin Media One

    Written & dir. by Cian Desmond. Produced by Caoilinn Handley. Cinematography by Jack Desmond.

    Watch the trailer.

  • Speak to Her (2019)

    Obafemi is an Eritrean bouncer in a Dublin nightclub. He tries to work up the courage to speak to Natka, a bathroom attendant, and decides to learn Polish to reach across the language divide.

    Winner, Best Short Form - Royal Television Society awards ROI 2019. Winner, Best Writing, RTS awards UK & ROI 2019.

    Dir. by Jack Desmond. Produced by Philip Emo. Edited by Cian Desmond.

    Watch the film.
